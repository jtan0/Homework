    Hi Everyone!

    I am freelance makeup artist currently living in Oklahoma City, OK.. I am obsessed with everything about makeup and beauty! I love the way makeup enhances your facial features and complexion to make you even more beautiful than you already are. Most of my inspiration comes from pictures, videos, and other artworks around me. Thanks to a wonderful support system, I am now ready to share my work with you.

    As you go through this site, you will see weekly product reviews, my experiences as a freelancer, and pictures of my artwork. . If you have any products you would like to share and for me to review, please email them to me! If you are interested in my work, head over to my contact page!
