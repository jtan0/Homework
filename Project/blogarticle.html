Mermaid Tutorial



I am so excited for Halloween! This year I decided to do a mythical mermaid look. I was inspired by the pictures on Pinterest and thought a mythical mermaid would be a great costume! Mermaids or in the myths “sirens” were known to be dangerous beautiful creatures that lure men with their singing. There are many stories about mermaids but I decided to go with the look in the myths. A colorful and bright face with gold flakes and of course you can’t forget about the scales. Here are the few easy steps to achieve my makeup look!

Step 1: Set your face with foundation and concealer. You should use a lighter concealer to highlight your face. Mermaids have that natural glow to their skin so you would want your face just as glowy! I used Loreal illuminator stick and added it with my concealer for the extra glow.

Step 2: Take a bright purple eyeshadow and start to contour your cheeks and forehead. You want to contour your face like how you would normally contour with a bronzer, but don’t be afraid to add a lot of eyeshadow! It is Halloween, the more makeup the better!

Step 3: Take a bright blue eyeshadow and go over some of the purple eyeshadow. I used the blue to contour my cheeks and down my neck. I also used some purple to contour down my neck and filled in the hollow areas of my collarbone. This will give more of a mermaid illusion.

Step 4: Now grab a pair of fish net stockings or whatever you have and use it as a template for the scales. I went to Walmart and bought a pair of cheap fish net stockings and just cut the fabric. I used a bright gold color from my Naked palette and packed the gold color over the fish net. You can apply your scales anywhere you want. I just applied it on my forehead, cheeks, and my neck area. I wanted my scales to be really shiny so I used a lot of the gold color!

Step 5: Fill in your eyebrows the way you normally would! I mean… mermaids have eyebrows too right? Anyways, I used the Anastasia dipbrow pomade and start to fill in my brows as usual.

Step 6: Grab a pair of your favorite lashes. I used some drugstore lashes by Kiss and applied it. I would recommend getting some dramatic lashes or some bottom lashes to make the makeup look more interesting. Then I used NYX gold glitter liner and lined the bottom of my eyes with the glitter. DO NOT BE SHY WITH THE GLITTER. Take some additional glitter and apply it all over your face. I got some Hard Candy glitter at the drugstore and dabbed it all over my face.

Step 7: Now time for extra highlight, lips, and the fun part...Rhinestones and gold flakes! I lined my lips with a purple eyeliner because I don’t have a purple lip liner… and just filled in my lips. I took the gold eyeshadow I used for my scales and highlight the bridge of my nose and the middle part of my lips. Finally, take some eyelash glue and start to glue the rhinestones and flakes on your face. You can apply it anywhere you want, I just applied it on the sides of my face and a little bit on my neck. Now… you’re a mythical mermaid!
