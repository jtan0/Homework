November 6, 2015

Ben Nye Banana Powder



Hello my loves! Today’s post will be a product review and some of my thoughts on this certain item. I recently purchased the Ben Nye Banana Powder because there were wonderful reviews on this product. I have to say this powder is great only on people with a darker skin tone. If you have fair skin then this is definitely not for you! This powder has a yellow tint in it and a little goes a long way! I note… THIS IS NOT FOR BAKING. If you are a makeup junkie then you are probably familiar with the term “baking”, this means setting your under eyes with powder for a few mins. I made the biggest mistake ever and let this powder sit for 10 mins letting it bake and what do you know… I had yellow rings under my eyes! It was NOT pretty. Luckily, I was able to fix it by wiping it off with some makeup remover and redo my concealer.

I have to be very careful with this powder and only use a small amount. Another mistake I made was packing the powder on my beauty blender and letting it cake under my eyes. That was a big NO NO. Oh well… at least I understand how this powder works now. I wouldn’t say this is my go-to product. It’s not bad but it is not my absolute favorite. I’m still a fan of translucent powders over the banana powders. With that being said, make sure this is right for your skin before consider purchasing it. I would recommend buying the small dome jars first to start off and see if you like it!
