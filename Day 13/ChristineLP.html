Brown Lips

I love dark lip colors, although I don’t wear it often, I am very particular of which color I choose. Dark colors can be tricky and may look different on everyone depending on your skin tone, however, but it never hurts to try it out and see if the a dark color looks good on you!

Since this topic is about dark lip colors, my obsession right now is Kylie Jenner’s brown lip. I saw the picture on her Instagram and just had to try it! Luckily, I was able to find a dark brown shade at Ulta! Their products tend to run out quick and I snatched the last NYX lip liner in Espresso and the NYX cream matte lipstick in London! Here is the final look… it is a little different than Kylie’s with a lighter shade and more of a chocolate brown, but I love it!

Products Used:

- NYX lip liner in Espresso
- NYX lip cream matte lipstick in London
