The Classic Fall Look with Teresa Vu

It was so much fun doing Teresaâ€™s makeup! Look how beautiful she is! This look can simply be an everyday wear or for a fun girls-night-out! For her makeup, I decided to do a classic fall look and used cool tones for the transformation.

Foundation: I used Revlon Colorstay in medium beige and applied it all over her face with a beauty blender.

Concealer: Conceal under-eye areas using a concealer brush and blending it out with a beauty blender. Remember ladies, if you want to really brighten and highlight your face, you must apply the concealer outwards in a downward triangular motion. Here is a simple face chart to show you how to highlight and contour the correct way.

Eyebrows: The Anastasia Brow Wiz is by far my favorite eyebrow pencil when it comes to having the perfect brows! I simply used the brow pencil in the shade medium brown and started to draw the shape following her natural brow line.

Eyeshadow: This is the fun part! I love the colors in the Carli Bybel palette! It is a must-have especially when you like the neutral shades and pigmented colors. For the eyes, I applied a brow bone color to the lid from the Carli palette and started blending it with a blending brush. Take a crease brush, find the hollow areas of the eyes and start to pack a taupe color to the outer-corners.

- NYX lip liner in Espresso
